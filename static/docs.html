Telemetry Diagnostic Glossary

Core algorithmic definitions and biological signal tracking breakdowns.

Beta/Alpha Ratio

A numerical index calculating active task engagement versus relative cognitive fatigue. A high ratio indicates intense focus or high situational stress, while a dropping ratio suggests a transition into relaxed states or processing exhaustion.

Alpha Power (Calm)

Reflects synchronized electrical neural oscillations in the 8–12 Hz frequency spectrum. Dominates during wakeful relaxation, deep mental visualization states, and baseline cognitive recovery.

Beta Power (Focus)

Tracks high-frequency brainwave patterns between 12–30 Hz. Associated with active processing, concrete problem solving, logical execution, and high attention-demanding workflows.

Continuous Biometric Telemetry

The raw mathematical engine processing incoming streams. Because physical capture arrays are awaiting configuration, the engine utilizes a continuous synthetic matrix to calculate relative standard deviations, generating live data nodes on the dashboard.
