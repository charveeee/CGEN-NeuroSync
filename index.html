Project Overview // v1.4.0

CGEN-NeuroSync

A mathematical neural visualizer and diagnostic platform designed to process raw biometric telemetry (There's over 400 indeces straight from PhysioNet). Built for decoding complex brainwave ratios and cognitive fatigue signals. There's also an option for Eye and Facial Tracking Telemetry!

⚡

Hardware Integration Status

Physical neural arrays are expensive and unaccessible to everyone so, this system is currently operating on an accurate mathematical simulation matrix to mimic live biometric activity without active hardware connection.
